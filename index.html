ABOUT TEAM

A group of passionate individuals who seeks to learn and explore the complex yet exciting field of computer science. From web and software development to solving complex algorithms, we aim to discover the rich world of computer science.

Our group believes in three fundamental values — competence, innovation, and collaboration. For we do not settle for the bare minimum but strive to do our fullest. From school, professional, or personal projects, we apply these values in everything we do.

One of our goals is to create a community where like-minded people can come to learn, share, and grow regardless of one's skills and experience. We encourage you to become a part of our ever-growing community.
